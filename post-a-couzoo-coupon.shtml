How to Post Your Coupon on CouZoo

To post your coupon on CouZou you must be a registerd mechant. If you have not yet registered you can Register here. It's quick, easy and free to register.

Once you are registereed and signed in click the "Create a Coupon" tab and simply follow the short, step-by-step form and that's it. You're coupon will begin to appear on CouZoo shortly after that.

At CouZoo you create your coupon, not us. You can use your images, write your own copy, and set the sales limits, dates, and restircitons that fit you and your business best. We don't tell you what to do. It's your business, it should be your coupon.
